ULTRA / Russian DBI: Game Download Service

Hunting down game files on download sites is a hassle. ULTRA (also known as ULTRANX or "Russian DBI") is a paid service that solves this — it works directly inside DBI on your Switch, lets you browse a library of games, and installs them straight to your console without you ever opening a browser.

Why people use it

  • No more searching nxbrew/ns2w for the right NSP
  • Browse and install from inside DBI on the Switch itself
  • Library is curated and kept up to date
  • Faster than chasing CDN downloads

What you need to know

  • It's paid. ULTRA is a subscription service. Pricing is on their site.
  • It's third-party. Not affiliated with PremazingPH. We don't control uptime, pricing, or library contents.
  • You need to register your Switch with their service to get access. The registration page walks you through it.
  • Use your judgement — like any third-party service, manage your expectations on availability and support.
