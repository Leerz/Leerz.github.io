2. Special Considerations for Android or Linux Installations

If you plan to install Android or Linux OS on your Switch, this must be done during the partitioning phase of the SD card setup. It cannot be done after the setup is complete. Attempting to add these OSes later will require starting over, so plan accordingly.

Final Notes

Upgrading or changing your SD card is an essential process for maintaining performance and storage needs. Always ensure you use high-quality SD cards to avoid compatibility or performance issues.
