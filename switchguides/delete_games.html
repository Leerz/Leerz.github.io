Delete Games

Deleting games from your jailbreak Switch is a simple process, but there are multiple methods available depending on your preference and the level of control you need. Below, we’ll walk you through three different ways to delete games from your device:

Method 1: Delete from the Home Screen

The easiest way to delete a game is directly from the Home screen:

  1. Highlight the game you want to delete.
  2. Press the + button on your Joy-Con or Pro controller.
  3. From the menu that appears, select Manage Software.
  4. Choose the Delete Software option.
  5. Confirm your selection to permanently delete the game.

Method 2: Delete from Settings

If you prefer to manage your data through the settings menu, you can delete games from there as well:

  1. Go to the Home Menu and select System Settings.
  2. Scroll down and select Data Management.
  3. Choose Manage Software to view a list of all installed games.
  4. Select the game you wish to delete, and choose the Delete Software option.
  5. Confirm your decision to remove the game from your device.

Method 3: Using Third-Party Applications (DBI)

If you want more control over which parts of a game are deleted, you can use third-party applications like DBI. This method allows you to delete specific components of a game, including the base game, updates, and DLCs:

  1. Launch DBI on your jailbreak Switch.
  2. Navigate to the list of installed titles on your device.
  3. Select the game you want to delete.
  4. Choose from the following options:
    • Delete Game to remove the entire title.
    • Delete Update to remove only the game’s update files.
    • Delete DLC to remove downloadable content.
  5. Confirm the deletion of the selected part of the game.

Conclusion

These methods give you flexibility in managing the games on your device. Whether you prefer a quick deletion from the Home screen, a more detailed approach through Settings, or complete control with third-party applications, each option will help you free up storage space and manage your Switch’s game library effectively.
