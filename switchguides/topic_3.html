Switch Homebrew Guide: Overview

This guide provides detailed information on various aspects of managing a jailbroken Nintendo Switch. The following sections tackle essential topics to help you customize and optimize your gaming experience while understanding the risks and best practices involved.

Themes, Cheats, Overclocking, and 60fps Patches

Discover how to personalize your Switch with themes, apply cheats, and enhance gameplay performance through overclocking and 60fps patches. This section discusses the benefits of these features, as well as potential risks, such as overheating, errors, or save data corruption. Use these features responsibly to avoid long-term issues.

Updating Firmware

Learn the best practices for updating your Switch firmware, including managing SD card files, keeping CFW/EMUMMC up-to-date, and handling Legit OS updates. This section emphasizes the importance of a "clean method" to avoid errors and maintain compatibility between your jailbreak tools and system updates.
