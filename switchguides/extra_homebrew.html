Themes and Cheats

Themes and cheats are popular customizations for the Switch, but they come with inherent risks. Improper installation or corruption can lead to system errors, save corruption, or interference with updates.

  • Themes: A corrupt or improperly installed theme can prevent your system from functioning correctly.

Overclocking with sys-clk

Overclocking your Switch using sys-clk can improve performance in demanding games, but it comes with significant risks. Overclocking increases power consumption and generates more heat, which can accelerate hardware degradation.

  • Heat Risks: Extended overclocking can cause overheating, shortening the lifespan of your console’s internal components.
  • Battery Drain: Overclocking leads to faster battery depletion during portable use.
  • System Instability: Using excessive overclocking settings may result in game crashes or system errors.

60fps patches allow certain games to run at higher frame rates, enhancing gameplay smoothness. However, these patches require caution:
