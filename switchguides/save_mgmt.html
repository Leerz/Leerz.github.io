Save Data Management

Managing save data is an essential aspect of using homebrew tools like DBI, JKSV, and Checkpoint on your Nintendo Switch. Below, we will cover several methods for managing your save data, whether you’re transferring, backing up, or restoring saves. We’ll also provide links to video guides for a better understanding of each method.

Using DBI with PC and MTP Responder

Follow these steps to manage your save data using DBI with your PC and MTP Responder:

  1. Connect your Switch to your PC via USB.
  2. Launch DBI from the homebrew menu on your Switch.
  3. From the DBI menu, select Run MTP Responder.
  4. On your PC, access your Switch’s drive by opening the DBI drive.
  5. Navigate to the 7: Saves folder to manage your save files.
  6. Copy the Installed games and Uninstalled games folders to your PC for backups.

DBI App Only (File Transfer)

To back up and restore save data using DBI:

  1. Open DBI and browse to the save data section.
  2. Highlight the game you want to back up, press the + button, and select Backup.
  3. To restore save data, press R to move between backups and select the file you want to restore.
